Parks in North & NW Boise

The City of Boise lists 46 parks in North & NW Boise, about 2,674 acres in total. 20 have playground equipment, 13 have a restroom that stays open through the winter, and 2 have a splash pad, fountain or pool. Every park below is scored on what matters with kids.

Best with kids in North & NW Boise

The highest Parent Scores in this part of town — the score weighs playground equipment, tree cover, open grass and restrooms.

  1. 1 Camel's Back Park 9/10 North End favorite: a playground with bonded rubber surfacing under mature trees, tennis courts, and the famous hill — kids climb it in summer and sled it after a good snow. Hyde Park's treats are a block away. Heated restrooms stay open all year.
  2. 2 Castle Hills Park 9/10 Northwest Boise sleeper: the playground was rebuilt in 2025 on bonded rubber, joined by tennis, pickleball, basketball, practice fields and an hourly dog off-leash area.
  3. 3 Kristin Armstrong Municipal Park 8.8/10 A shady 28 acres on the Boise River near the East End — reservable picnic areas make it a birthday-party staple, and heated restrooms are open year-round.
  4. 4 Julia Davis Park 8.5/10 You can stack a whole day here: Zoo Boise, the Rose Garden, the bandshell and the Boise River all live inside the park, with the best mature shade in town. Year-round restrooms make it a reliable winter outing too.
  5. 5 Ann Morrison Park 8.3/10 Boise's big classic park. The interactive fountain (Memorial Day–Labor Day, sunrise to sunset) is a summer ritual — bring towels and a change of clothes. The playground has bonded rubber surfacing, and the huge lawns absorb any amount of energy.

Common questions

How many parks are there in North & NW Boise?

The City of Boise lists 46 parks in North & NW Boise, covering about 2,674 acres. 20 of them have playground equipment.

Which park in North & NW Boise is best with young kids?

Camel's Back Park has the highest Parent Score in North & NW Boise at 9 out of 10, based on its playground, tree cover, open grass and restrooms. The full ranking for the area is above.

Which parks in North & NW Boise have restrooms open in winter?

13 of the 46 parks in North & NW Boise have heated restrooms that stay open year-round: Camel's Back Park, Kristin Armstrong Municipal Park, Julia Davis Park, Ann Morrison Park, Esther Simplot Park, Veterans Memorial Park, Fort Boise Park, Shoreline Park, C.W. Moore Park, Kathryn Albertson Park, Marianne Williams Park, Warm Springs Park, Hillside to Hollow Reserve.

Are there splash pads in North & NW Boise?

2 parks in North & NW Boise have water play: Ann Morrison Park, Fairview Park. Water features run Memorial Day through Labor Day.

Which parks in North & NW Boise have the most shade?

20 parks are rated leafy in North & NW Boise, meaning dense mature tree cover measured from the city tree inventory: Camel's Back Park, Castle Hills Park, Kristin Armstrong Municipal Park, Julia Davis Park, Catalpa Park, Eagle Rock Park, Sunset Park, Elm Grove Park and others.

Parent notes

Common questions about Boise Hills Park

Does Boise Hills Park have a restroom?

No. Boise Hills Park has no restroom at any time of year.

Does Boise Hills Park have a playground?

Yes. Boise Hills Park has equipment for both toddlers (ages 2–5) and big kids (ages 5–12), including net climber, slides, swings. The surfacing is bonded rubber, which is stroller and wheelchair friendly. The newest structure was installed in 2011.

Is there shade at Boise Hills Park?

Boise Hills Park has some shade — a mix of trees and open lawn. The city tree inventory lists 60 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Boise Hills Park?

On-street parking.

How big is Boise Hills Park?

Boise Hills Park is 7.3 acres. It is classified by the city as a neighborhood park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 651 E Clubview Dr.

For the city's official write-up and any current alerts, see the Boise Hills Park page at Boise Parks and Recreation.

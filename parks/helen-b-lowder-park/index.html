Common questions about Helen B. Lowder Park

Does Helen B. Lowder Park have a restroom?

Helen B. Lowder Park has a restroom that is open in the warm months. The building is winterized for the cold months, but the city leaves a portable toilet on site.

Does Helen B. Lowder Park have a playground?

Yes. Helen B. Lowder Park has equipment for both toddlers (ages 2–5) and big kids (ages 5–12), including slides. The surfacing is wood chips. The newest structure was installed in 1999.

Is there shade at Helen B. Lowder Park?

Helen B. Lowder Park has some shade — a mix of trees and open lawn. The city tree inventory lists 74 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Helen B. Lowder Park?

Small lot by the courts.

How big is Helen B. Lowder Park?

Helen B. Lowder Park is 5.1 acres. It is classified by the city as a neighborhood park and is located in the Southeast area of Boise at 3450 S Law Ave.

For the city's official write-up and any current alerts, see the Helen B. Lowder Park page at Boise Parks and Recreation.

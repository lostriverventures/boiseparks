Parkcenter Park

385 E Parkcenter Blvd, Boise, ID 83706 · 14.8 acres · Southeast

Parent notes

Pond park in East Boise that fills with waterfowl in spring and summer — bring binoculars. Year-round restroom; street parking.

Common questions about Parkcenter Park

Does Parkcenter Park have a restroom?

Yes. Parkcenter Park has a heated restroom building that stays open year-round, including through the winter.

Does Parkcenter Park have a playground?

No. Parkcenter Park has no playground equipment.

Is there shade at Parkcenter Park?

Parkcenter Park has some shade — a mix of trees and open lawn. The city tree inventory lists 85 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Parkcenter Park?

On-street parking.

How big is Parkcenter Park?

Parkcenter Park is 14.8 acres. It is classified by the city as a special use park and is located in the Southeast area of Boise at 385 E Parkcenter Blvd.

For the city's official write-up and any current alerts, see the Parkcenter Park page at Boise Parks and Recreation.

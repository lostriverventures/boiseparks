Common questions about Fairview Park

Does Fairview Park have a restroom?

Fairview Park has a seasonal restroom. It is open in the warm months and closed for the winter, with no portable toilet as backup.

Does Fairview Park have a playground?

Yes. Fairview Park has equipment for both toddlers (ages 2–5) and big kids (ages 5–12), including merry-go-round / spinner, net climber, slides, swings. The surfacing is bonded rubber, which is stroller and wheelchair friendly. The newest structure was installed in 2025.

Does Fairview Park have a splash pad?

Fairview Park has a misters. It runs sunrise–sunset, memorial day–labor day and is free to use.

Is there shade at Fairview Park?

Fairview Park is rated leafy, meaning dense mature tree cover. The city tree inventory lists 49 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Fairview Park?

On-street parking.

How big is Fairview Park?

Fairview Park is 2.3 acres. It is classified by the city as a neighborhood park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 2300 W Idaho St.

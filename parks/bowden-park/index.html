Common questions about Bowden Park

Does Bowden Park have a restroom?

Bowden Park has a seasonal restroom. It is open in the warm months and closed for the winter, with no portable toilet as backup.

Does Bowden Park have a playground?

Yes. Bowden Park has equipment for big kids (ages 5–12), including slides. The surfacing is bonded rubber, which is stroller and wheelchair friendly. The newest structure was installed in 2022.

Is there shade at Bowden Park?

Bowden Park is rated leafy, meaning dense mature tree cover. The city tree inventory lists 52 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Bowden Park?

On-street parking.

How big is Bowden Park?

Bowden Park is 2.7 acres. It is classified by the city as a neighborhood park and is located in the Central Bench area of Boise at 3230 W Edson St.

For the city's official write-up and any current alerts, see the Bowden Park page at Boise Parks and Recreation.

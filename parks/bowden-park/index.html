About Bowden Park

Bowden Park is a 3-acre park located next to South Junior High School. Park features picnic tables, a playground, outdoor gym, a restroom and open play areas. Boise Parks and Recreation is committed to providing accessible and inclusive experiences for users of all abilities. The playground at Bowden Park includes Avalanche Inclusive Slide and Ten Spin and has bonded rubber surfacing for mobility device users.

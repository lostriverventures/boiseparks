Common questions about Mountain View Park

Does Mountain View Park have a restroom?

Mountain View Park has a seasonal restroom. It is open in the warm months and closed for the winter, with no portable toilet as backup.

Does Mountain View Park have a playground?

Yes. Mountain View Park has equipment for toddlers (ages 2–5), including slides, swings. The surfacing is wood chips. The newest structure was installed in 2011.

Is there shade at Mountain View Park?

Mountain View Park has some shade — a mix of trees and open lawn. The city tree inventory lists 68 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Mountain View Park?

On-street parking.

How big is Mountain View Park?

Mountain View Park is 8.7 acres. It is classified by the city as a neighborhood park and is located in the West Bench area of Boise at 7006 W Ustick Rd.

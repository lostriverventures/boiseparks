About Rhodes Park

Rhodes Skate Park is a 1.28-acre special use park located in Downtown Boise near the Linen District. Local youth helped design the skate park that caters to skateboarding and inline skating. By order of the Director: No motorized vehicles or electric mobility devices allowed in City of Boise skate parks. All alcoholic beverages, including beer and wine, are prohibited at Rhodes Skate Park.

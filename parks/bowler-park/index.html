About Bowler Park

Bowler Park is a 24.62 acre park in Southeast Boise near Surprise Valley. Amenities include bocce courts, playground, fenced dog off-leash area, disc golf course, outdoor gym, skate spot, restrooms and misters. This property was acquired from Beth and Bruce Bowler in 1995. Boise Parks and Recreation is committed to providing accessible and inclusive experiences for users of all abilities. The playground at Bowler Park includes a Volta® Inclusive Spinner and has bonded rubber surfacing for mobility device users. Take a video tour of this playground by clicking the button below.

Warm Springs Park

1811 E Warm Springs Ave, Boise, ID 83712 · 20.2 acres · North River

Parent notes

Riverside open space in the East End with Greenbelt access, birding and a native plant garden — plus a year-round restroom. More nature ramble than playground (there isn't one).

Common questions about Warm Springs Park

Does Warm Springs Park have a restroom?

Yes. Warm Springs Park has a heated restroom building that stays open year-round, including through the winter.

Does Warm Springs Park have a playground?

No. Warm Springs Park has no playground equipment.

Is there shade at Warm Springs Park?

Warm Springs Park is mostly full sun, with little tree cover.

Where do you park at Warm Springs Park?

On-street parking.

How big is Warm Springs Park?

Warm Springs Park is 20.2 acres. It is classified by the city as a special use park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 1811 E Warm Springs Ave.

For the city's official write-up and any current alerts, see the Warm Springs Park page at Boise Parks and Recreation.

Optimist Youth Sports Complex

9889 W Hill Rd, Boise, ID 83714 · 52.5 acres · North River

Parent notes

Weekend-soccer headquarters in northwest Boise, with a fishing pond and public-art pieces to wander past. Note the seasonal pet restrictions — and there's no playground.

Common questions about Optimist Youth Sports Complex

Does Optimist Youth Sports Complex have a restroom?

Optimist Youth Sports Complex has a restroom that is open in the warm months. The building is winterized for the cold months, but the city leaves a portable toilet on site.

Does Optimist Youth Sports Complex have a playground?

No. Optimist Youth Sports Complex has no playground equipment.

Is there shade at Optimist Youth Sports Complex?

Optimist Youth Sports Complex has some shade — a mix of trees and open lawn. The city tree inventory lists 248 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Optimist Youth Sports Complex?

Large complex lots.

How big is Optimist Youth Sports Complex?

Optimist Youth Sports Complex is 52.5 acres. It is classified by the city as a community park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 9889 W Hill Rd.

For the city's official write-up and any current alerts, see the Optimist Youth Sports Complex page at Boise Parks and Recreation.

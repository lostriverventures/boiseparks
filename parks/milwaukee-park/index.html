Common questions about Milwaukee Park

Does Milwaukee Park have a restroom?

Milwaukee Park has a seasonal restroom. It is open in the warm months and closed for the winter, with no portable toilet as backup.

Does Milwaukee Park have a playground?

No. Milwaukee Park has no playground equipment.

Is there shade at Milwaukee Park?

Milwaukee Park is mostly full sun, with little tree cover. The city tree inventory lists 10 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Milwaukee Park?

On-street parking.

How big is Milwaukee Park?

Milwaukee Park is 10 acres. It is classified by the city as a special use park and is located in the West Bench area of Boise at 3950 N Milwaukee St.

Marianne Williams Park

3451 E Barber Valley Dr, Boise, ID 83716 · 74.1 acres · North River

Parent notes

Big open riverside park in Barber Valley with two large ponds and flat paved paths that are perfect for strollers. No playground yet, and bikes aren't allowed in the interior — it's a walking park.

Common questions about Marianne Williams Park

Does Marianne Williams Park have a restroom?

Yes. Marianne Williams Park has a heated restroom building that stays open year-round, including through the winter.

Does Marianne Williams Park have a playground?

No. Marianne Williams Park has no playground equipment.

Is there shade at Marianne Williams Park?

Marianne Williams Park is mostly full sun, with little tree cover. The city tree inventory lists 244 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Marianne Williams Park?

Lot off E Barber Valley Dr.

How big is Marianne Williams Park?

Marianne Williams Park is 74.1 acres. It is classified by the city as a special use park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 3451 E Barber Valley Dr.

For the city's official write-up and any current alerts, see the Marianne Williams Park page at Boise Parks and Recreation.

Barber Observation Point

6300 S Surprise Way, Boise, ID 83716 · 2.2 acres · Southeast

Parent notes

Not a play park — a short walking path, interpretive signs and a big view over the Barber Pool Conservation Area. Has its own small lot; no restrooms.

Common questions about Barber Observation Point

Does Barber Observation Point have a restroom?

No. Barber Observation Point has no restroom at any time of year.

Does Barber Observation Point have a playground?

No. Barber Observation Point has no playground equipment.

Is there shade at Barber Observation Point?

Barber Observation Point has some shade — a mix of trees and open lawn. The city tree inventory lists 14 trees in the park.

How big is Barber Observation Point?

Barber Observation Point is 2.2 acres. It is classified by the city as a special use park and is located in the Southeast area of Boise at 6300 S Surprise Way.

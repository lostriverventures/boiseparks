Camel's Back Park

1200 W Heron St, Boise, ID 83702 · 11.3 acres · North River

Parent notes

North End favorite: a playground with bonded rubber surfacing under mature trees, tennis courts, and the famous hill — kids climb it in summer and sled it after a good snow. Hyde Park's treats are a block away. Heated restrooms stay open all year.

Common questions about Camel's Back Park

Does Camel's Back Park have a restroom?

Yes. Camel's Back Park has a heated restroom building that stays open year-round, including through the winter.

Does Camel's Back Park have a playground?

Yes. Camel's Back Park has equipment for both toddlers (ages 2–5) and big kids (ages 5–12), including slides, swings, track ride / zipline. The surfacing is bonded rubber, which is stroller and wheelchair friendly. The newest structure was installed in 2000.

Is there shade at Camel's Back Park?

Camel's Back Park is rated leafy, meaning dense mature tree cover. The city tree inventory lists 151 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Camel's Back Park?

Small lot off 13th St fills fast on weekends — overflow is neighborhood street parking.

How big is Camel's Back Park?

Camel's Back Park is 11.3 acres. It is classified by the city as a neighborhood park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 1200 W Heron St.

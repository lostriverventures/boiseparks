Common questions about Idaho Fallen Firefighter Memorial

Does Idaho Fallen Firefighter Memorial have a restroom?

Idaho Fallen Firefighter Memorial has a seasonal restroom. It is open in the warm months and closed for the winter, with no portable toilet as backup.

Does Idaho Fallen Firefighter Memorial have a playground?

No. Idaho Fallen Firefighter Memorial has no playground equipment.

Is there shade at Idaho Fallen Firefighter Memorial?

Idaho Fallen Firefighter Memorial is rated leafy, meaning dense mature tree cover. The city tree inventory lists 80 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Idaho Fallen Firefighter Memorial?

On-street parking.

How big is Idaho Fallen Firefighter Memorial?

Idaho Fallen Firefighter Memorial is 4.3 acres. It is classified by the city as a linear park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 1775 W Shoreline Dr.

For the city's official write-up and any current alerts, see the Idaho Fallen Firefighter Memorial page at Boise Parks and Recreation.

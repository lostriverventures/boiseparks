About Veterans Memorial Park

Veterans Memorial Park is located at 930 N. Veterans Memorial Parkway. Amenities include several memorials throughout the park, a plaza, playground, access to the Boise River Greenbelt, open play areas, restrooms and reservable picnic area and shelter. In Veterans Memorial Park pond, service animals welcome. No pets per city code.

Mariposa Park

9851 W Irving St, Boise, ID 83704 · 8.1 acres · West Bench

Parent notes

A nature-walk park: pond, paths, a pollinator garden with monarch-butterfly signage and a fenced dog area — but no playground.

Common questions about Mariposa Park

Does Mariposa Park have a restroom?

Mariposa Park has a restroom that is open in the warm months. The building is winterized for the cold months, but the city leaves a portable toilet on site.

Does Mariposa Park have a playground?

No. Mariposa Park has no playground equipment.

Is there shade at Mariposa Park?

Mariposa Park has some shade — a mix of trees and open lawn. The city tree inventory lists 141 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Mariposa Park?

Small lot at the northeast corner.

How big is Mariposa Park?

Mariposa Park is 8.1 acres. It is classified by the city as a neighborhood park and is located in the West Bench area of Boise at 9851 W Irving St.

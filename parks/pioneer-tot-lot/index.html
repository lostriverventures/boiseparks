Common questions about Pioneer Tot Lot

Does Pioneer Tot Lot have a restroom?

No. Pioneer Tot Lot has no restroom at any time of year.

Does Pioneer Tot Lot have a playground?

Yes. Pioneer Tot Lot has equipment for both toddlers (ages 2–5) and big kids (ages 5–12), including slides. The surfacing is wood chips. The newest structure was installed in 1999.

Is there shade at Pioneer Tot Lot?

Pioneer Tot Lot is mostly full sun, with little tree cover.

Where do you park at Pioneer Tot Lot?

Metered street parking & paid garages nearby.

How big is Pioneer Tot Lot?

Pioneer Tot Lot is 0.1 acres. It is classified by the city as a special use park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 1139 W Grand Ave.

For the city's official write-up and any current alerts, see the Pioneer Tot Lot page at Boise Parks and Recreation.

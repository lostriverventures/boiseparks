Parent notes

A 0.4-acre pocket lawn under mature trees in East Boise — fine for a blanket picnic, but there's no playground, restroom or parking.

Common questions about Aldape Park

Does Aldape Park have a restroom?

No. Aldape Park has no restroom at any time of year.

Does Aldape Park have a playground?

No. Aldape Park has no playground equipment.

Is there shade at Aldape Park?

Aldape Park is rated leafy, meaning dense mature tree cover. The city tree inventory lists 7 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Aldape Park?

On-street parking.

How big is Aldape Park?

Aldape Park is 0.4 acres. It is classified by the city as a mini park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 629 N San Jose Dr.

Common questions about Hillside to Hollow Reserve

Does Hillside to Hollow Reserve have a restroom?

Yes. Hillside to Hollow Reserve has a heated restroom building that stays open year-round, including through the winter.

Does Hillside to Hollow Reserve have a playground?

No. Hillside to Hollow Reserve has no playground equipment.

Is there shade at Hillside to Hollow Reserve?

Hillside to Hollow Reserve is mostly full sun, with little tree cover. The city tree inventory lists 10 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Hillside to Hollow Reserve?

Small trailhead lots — fill early on weekends.

How big is Hillside to Hollow Reserve?

Hillside to Hollow Reserve is 269.2 acres. It is classified by the city as a open space park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 2700 W Hill Rd.

For the city's official write-up and any current alerts, see the Hillside to Hollow Reserve page at Boise Parks and Recreation.

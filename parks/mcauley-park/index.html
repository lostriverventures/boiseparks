Common questions about McAuley Park

Does McAuley Park have a restroom?

No. McAuley Park has no restroom at any time of year.

Does McAuley Park have a playground?

No. McAuley Park has no playground equipment.

Is there shade at McAuley Park?

McAuley Park is rated leafy, meaning dense mature tree cover. The city tree inventory lists 6 trees in the park.

Where do you park at McAuley Park?

On-street parking.

How big is McAuley Park?

McAuley Park is 0.3 acres. It is classified by the city as a mini park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 1655 W Resseguie St.

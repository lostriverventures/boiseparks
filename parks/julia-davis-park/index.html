Julia Davis Park

700 N Capitol Blvd, Boise, ID 83702 · 90.4 acres · North River

Parent notes

You can stack a whole day here: Zoo Boise, the Rose Garden, the bandshell and the Boise River all live inside the park, with the best mature shade in town. Year-round restrooms make it a reliable winter outing too.

Common questions about Julia Davis Park

Does Julia Davis Park have a restroom?

Yes. Julia Davis Park has a heated restroom building that stays open year-round, including through the winter.

Does Julia Davis Park have a playground?

Yes. Julia Davis Park has equipment for toddlers (ages 2–5), including slides, swings. The surfacing is wood chips. The newest structure was installed in 1996.

Is there shade at Julia Davis Park?

Julia Davis Park is rated leafy, meaning dense mature tree cover. The city tree inventory lists 929 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Julia Davis Park?

Several lots along Julia Davis Drive inside the park; fills during zoo hours and events.

How big is Julia Davis Park?

Julia Davis Park is 90.4 acres. It is classified by the city as a regional park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 700 N Capitol Blvd.

For the city's official write-up and any current alerts, see the Julia Davis Park page at Boise Parks and Recreation.

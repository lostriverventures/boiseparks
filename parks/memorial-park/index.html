Common questions about Memorial Park

Does Memorial Park have a restroom?

Memorial Park has a seasonal restroom. It is open in the warm months and closed for the winter, with no portable toilet as backup.

Does Memorial Park have a playground?

Yes. Memorial Park has equipment for both toddlers (ages 2–5) and big kids (ages 5–12), including slides, swings. The surfacing is wood chips. The newest structure was installed in 2001.

Is there shade at Memorial Park?

Memorial Park is rated leafy, meaning dense mature tree cover. The city tree inventory lists 100 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Memorial Park?

On-street parking.

How big is Memorial Park?

Memorial Park is 4.9 acres. It is classified by the city as a neighborhood park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 900 N 6 Th St.

JA KAFF Boise Whitewater Park

3400 W Pleasonton Ave, Boise, ID 83701 · 1.7 acres · North River

Parent notes

Watch kayakers and river surfers ride the adjustable wave — grade-schoolers find it mesmerizing. Park in the Esther Simplot / Quinn's Pond lots.

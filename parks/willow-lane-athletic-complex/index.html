About Willow Lane Athletic Complex

Willow Lane Athletic Complex is a 57-acre, special use park, adjacent to the Boise River Greenbelt. The athletic complex features several softball fields, a soccer field, playground and dirt jump park. Service animals welcome. No pets per city code in Willow Lane Athletic Complex from March 1-Oct. 31. November 1-February 28 dogs are permitted but must be on a leash. Please pick up after your dog.

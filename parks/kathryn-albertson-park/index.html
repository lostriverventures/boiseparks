Kathryn Albertson Park

1001 N Americana Blvd, Boise, ID 83706 · 41.4 acres · North River

Parent notes

No playground here — this one is for wildlife walks. Wide paved loops are perfect for strollers and new walkers, and ducks and other waterfowl are basically guaranteed. Swimming, wading, biking and scooters are not allowed; it stays calm.

Common questions about Kathryn Albertson Park

Does Kathryn Albertson Park have a restroom?

Yes. Kathryn Albertson Park has a heated restroom building that stays open year-round, including through the winter.

Does Kathryn Albertson Park have a playground?

No. Kathryn Albertson Park has no playground equipment.

Is there shade at Kathryn Albertson Park?

Kathryn Albertson Park has some shade — a mix of trees and open lawn. The city tree inventory lists 732 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Kathryn Albertson Park?

Lot at the Americana Blvd entrance.

How big is Kathryn Albertson Park?

Kathryn Albertson Park is 41.4 acres. It is classified by the city as a special use park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 1001 N Americana Blvd.

Grove Plaza

827 W Main St, Boise, ID 83705 · 1.3 acres · Downtown

Parent notes

Downtown's interactive fountain (8 a.m.–11 p.m., Memorial Day–Labor Day) is a classic run-through-the-jets stop after the library or Saturday market. No restrooms or playground — it's a plaza.

Common questions about Grove Plaza

Does Grove Plaza have a restroom?

No. Grove Plaza has no restroom at any time of year.

Does Grove Plaza have a playground?

No. Grove Plaza has no playground equipment.

Does Grove Plaza have a splash pad?

Grove Plaza has a interactive fountain. It runs 8 a.m.–11 p.m., memorial day–labor day and is free to use.

Is there shade at Grove Plaza?

Grove Plaza is rated leafy, meaning dense mature tree cover. The city tree inventory lists 33 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Grove Plaza?

Metered street parking & paid garages nearby.

How big is Grove Plaza?

Grove Plaza is 1.3 acres. It is classified by the city as a special use park and is located in the Downtown area of Boise at 827 W Main St.

For the city's official write-up and any current alerts, see the Grove Plaza page at Boise Parks and Recreation.

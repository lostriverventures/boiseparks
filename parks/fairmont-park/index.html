Common questions about Fairmont Park

Does Fairmont Park have a restroom?

Fairmont Park has a restroom that is open in the warm months. The building is winterized for the cold months, but the city leaves a portable toilet on site.

Does Fairmont Park have a playground?

Yes. Fairmont Park has equipment for both toddlers (ages 2–5) and big kids (ages 5–12), including net climber, slides, swings. The surfacing is wood chips. The newest structure was installed in 2009.

Does Fairmont Park have a swimming pool?

Yes. Fairmont Park has a city-run outdoor pool. Its season, hours and admission are listed on the City of Boise page for the park.

Is there shade at Fairmont Park?

Fairmont Park is rated leafy, meaning dense mature tree cover. The city tree inventory lists 118 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Fairmont Park?

Lots off Northview St.

How big is Fairmont Park?

Fairmont Park is 4.2 acres. It is classified by the city as a special use park and is located in the West Bench area of Boise at 7925 W Northview St.

For the city's official write-up and any current alerts, see the Fairmont Park page at Boise Parks and Recreation.

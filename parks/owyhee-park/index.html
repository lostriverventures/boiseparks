Common questions about Owyhee Park

Does Owyhee Park have a restroom?

Owyhee Park has a restroom that is open in the warm months. The building is winterized for the cold months, but the city leaves a portable toilet on site.

Does Owyhee Park have a playground?

Yes. Owyhee Park has equipment for both toddlers (ages 2–5) and big kids (ages 5–12), including slides, swings. The surfacing is wood chips. The newest structure was installed in 2002.

Is there shade at Owyhee Park?

Owyhee Park is rated leafy, meaning dense mature tree cover. The city tree inventory lists 81 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Owyhee Park?

On-street parking.

How big is Owyhee Park?

Owyhee Park is 5 acres. It is classified by the city as a neighborhood park and is located in the Central Bench area of Boise at 3400 W Elder St.

For the city's official write-up and any current alerts, see the Owyhee Park page at Boise Parks and Recreation.

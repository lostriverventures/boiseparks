Boise Parks / Eagle Rock Park

Eagle Rock Park

2150 E Old Penitentiary Rd, Boise, ID 83712 · 8.9 acres · North River

Parent notes

East End park near the Old Penitentiary with an all-ages playground, reservable shelter and courts — a solid backup when the river parks are packed.

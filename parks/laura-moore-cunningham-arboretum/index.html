Common questions about Laura Moore Cunningham Arboretum

Does Laura Moore Cunningham Arboretum have a restroom?

No. Laura Moore Cunningham Arboretum has no restroom at any time of year.

Does Laura Moore Cunningham Arboretum have a playground?

No. Laura Moore Cunningham Arboretum has no playground equipment.

Is there shade at Laura Moore Cunningham Arboretum?

Laura Moore Cunningham Arboretum is mostly full sun, with little tree cover.

Where do you park at Laura Moore Cunningham Arboretum?

On-street parking.

How big is Laura Moore Cunningham Arboretum?

Laura Moore Cunningham Arboretum is 4.8 acres. It is classified by the city as a special use park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 1149 E Lewis St.

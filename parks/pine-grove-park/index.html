About Pine Grove Park

Current amenities include a fenced dog park, open play area, half-court basketball court, two shelters, playground and a restroom. Boise Parks and Recreation is committed to providing accessible and inclusive experiences for users of all abilities. The playground at Pine Grove Park includes an inclusive swing seat and has bonded rubber surfacing for mobility device users. Take a video tour of this playground by clicking the button below.

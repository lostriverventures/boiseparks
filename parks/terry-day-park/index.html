About Terry Day Park

Park amenities include a gravel pathway, playground, restroom, picnic shelter, fishing pond, tennis courts, open grassy areas, two parking lots, mature and newly planted trees and picnic benches. Boise Parks and Recreation is committed to providing accessible and inclusive experiences for users of all abilities. The playground at Terry Day Park has bonded rubber surfacing for mobility device users.

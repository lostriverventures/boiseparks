Hillside Park

4150 N 36Th St, Boise, ID 83703 · 11 acres · North River

Parent notes

A ballfield park below the foothills: a lighted softball diamond, youth diamonds and even a spot for horses. It's a game-day place — no playground here.

Common questions about Hillside Park

Does Hillside Park have a restroom?

Hillside Park has a seasonal restroom. It is open in the warm months and closed for the winter, with no portable toilet as backup.

Does Hillside Park have a playground?

No. Hillside Park has no playground equipment.

Is there shade at Hillside Park?

Hillside Park has some shade — a mix of trees and open lawn. The city tree inventory lists 35 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Hillside Park?

Lot shared with Hillside Junior High next door.

How big is Hillside Park?

Hillside Park is 11 acres. It is classified by the city as a community park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 4150 N 36Th St.

For the city's official write-up and any current alerts, see the Hillside Park page at Boise Parks and Recreation.

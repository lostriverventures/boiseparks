Common questions about Gordon S. Bowen Park

Does Gordon S. Bowen Park have a restroom?

No. Gordon S. Bowen Park has no restroom at any time of year.

Does Gordon S. Bowen Park have a playground?

Yes. Gordon S. Bowen Park has equipment for both toddlers (ages 2–5) and big kids (ages 5–12), including slides. The surfacing is wood chips. The newest structure was installed in 2005.

Is there shade at Gordon S. Bowen Park?

Gordon S. Bowen Park is rated leafy, meaning dense mature tree cover. The city tree inventory lists 20 trees in the park.

Where do you park at Gordon S. Bowen Park?

On-street parking.

How big is Gordon S. Bowen Park?

Gordon S. Bowen Park is 0.3 acres. It is classified by the city as a mini park and is located in the North River area of Boise at 1013 W O'Farrell St.
